5 digital marketing tips that can help you generate more leads for your website

It is always a joy for a web owner whenever he gets the chance to convert a casual visitor into a potential sales lead. This is even more essential for an online business that has to depend solely on its own web performances to succeed. Any online business owner always strives to improve his/her conversion rates in order to succeed. 

 As presented in simple terms in the below diagram, conversion rate is the ability of converting casual visitors on a website into potential leads for the online business. The list of what a conversion might include at the end is endless. It can include a once off customer turning into a loyal customer, a casual visitor who decides to sign up on your email lists, customers who constantly make inquiries on your products and services, and even active customers who are constantly posting comments. 

Being able to convert visitors into leads is not a cakewalk. As a matter of fact, up to date, there are numerous web companies out there which are still struggling to achieve this. To be able to achieve it, an individual needs to come up with a flawless conversion strategy, keep testing and analyzing the same to ensure that it is spot on. 

This whole effort demands the use of basic marketing techniques and knowledge. As I always like to illustrate things and make them more comprehensible, in this case I would like you to picture yourself as a shop owner (not an e-shop owner) in a high end shopping area. You will be getting a lot of people entering your shop and even more just passing by. However, this does not mean that your sales will be the optimum as these people might not find anything interesting about your shop. To achieve near optimum sales capacity you have to convert your shop visitors into actual customers. Therefore, you have to find ways to make your shop interesting and attractive to visitors and those passing by. The same stands for the case of a corporate website. If your casual visitors do not find anything interesting then the lead will never occur. 

 Important tips that can help you improve your lead conversion rates: 

 Boosting your lead conversion rates can be a decisive factor for the success of your website. To help you achieve this I give you here below 5 tips which can be easily applied and could help you boost your lead conversion rates. 

 More-Leads

1) Apply different strategies to meet the needs of certain types of leads 

To succeed with your conversion rates, treat the leads individually. The main reason attributed to this is that your customer base is likely to be composed of different groups of people with different interests and triggers. You may have customers who prefer reading exhaustive content while others may lack the patient and like to take part in a webinar. As a web owner, you need to consider this diversity and take care of it. 

2) Offer incentives 

You can increase your lead conversion rate by offering incentives to your visitors. This method has been in use for quite a while now due to the fact that it has been tested and found to be successful. The method is best practiced when visitors are asked by bloggers to submit their emails and in return given an exclusive link to free resource eBook as the incentive. For lead capturing purposes, this principle works wonders! 

3) Get personal with your leads 

This entails taking time to know your leads better. Before coming up with your own content marketing strategy, make sure you familiarize yourself with your customers. Start by identifying their demographics, income, occupation, lifestyle, buying habits and any other related data. To most people, this data is referred to as Central Demographic Model (CDM). It is also very important to familiarize yourself with the Central Psychopathic Model (CPM). The CPM defines the preferences and the behaviors affecting the buyers’ decisions. 

4) Learn about the conversion process 

Understanding the conversion rate process can contribute a lot towards lead generation. The process is not science parse. The conversion process is actually a hit and trial work. Analyse your past and identify areas that might have led to successful conversion rates in the past. Understand what has impacted your customers buying decisions. Once this is done, you can then incorporate it into your marketing model. 

5) Make it a habit to always customize follow up communication 

Ensure that all your follow-ups are customized to meet the client’s preferences. This should be done regardless of your strategy. It is very important to ensure that as a business, you have proper understanding of your follow up campaign. By simply understanding your follow up campaign, you will be in a better position of customizing your campaigns so as to avoid any clashes with other different follow up campaigns. Incorporating follow up measures into your lead generation campaigns and content marketing will improve your conversion rates.
