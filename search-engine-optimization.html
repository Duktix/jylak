Types of Seo Search Engine Optimization

According to the Seo site, optimization of the site is divided into two types: On-site optimization of the site and off-site optimization of a site (on and off-site). Benefits of On Page Optimization are that a guaranteed result is achieved, both quickly and inexpensively. The drawbacks of Off Page Optimization are that the result comes slowly, is expensive and has no full guarantee.

On Page Seo Search Engine Optimization - OnPage (Inside Site)

Internal site optimization is usually optimized for website structure, text, URLs, and HTML code so that each page is positioned as high as possible on search engines. 

If applied alone OnPage Seo is suitable for niche and niche markets with low to medium competition. Note that this service does not include link purchasing, which is the most expensive part of optimization and therefore low prices are possible. 

Naturally, these prices are not relevant to areas with the highest competition, such as forex, property, design, hosting, money, and more. There will not be the best results just in this way and optimization will have to be done in combination with:: Off Page Seo

Off Page Seo Search Engine Optimization, OffPage

OffPage Seo means that it is optimized outside your site, working on other sites. This type of Seo optimization, packed with the right OnPage Seo optimization, gives even higher results, but it is also more expensive. Buying links, posting articles and links, linking bridging and other ways to promote your site and make it a top position on Google.

According to the ways to achieve the result, optimizing the SEO site optimization is divided into three types: Gray hat SEO, Black hat SEO, and White hat SEO. The difference is whether or not the relevant methods are enabled by the search engines. Black hat means prohibited methods, with Gray hat - semi forbidden, and White hat are the allowed techniques.
